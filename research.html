Innovation & Technology

Cutting-edge platforms driving our research and development

Genomics & Precision Medicine

Leveraging advanced genomic technologies to identify novel targets and develop personalized therapies tailored to individual genetic profiles. Our genomics platform integrates multi-omics data to uncover disease mechanisms and biomarkers.

AI-Driven Drug Discovery

Employing artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to accelerate drug discovery, predict drug-target interactions, optimize molecular structures, and identify potential therapeutic candidates with greater efficiency.

Advanced Screening Platforms

Utilizing high-throughput screening technologies and phenotypic assays to evaluate thousands of compounds rapidly. Our proprietary screening platforms incorporate human cell-based models and organ-on-chip technology for improved predictability.

Novel Drug Delivery Systems

Developing innovative drug delivery technologies to enhance therapeutic efficacy, improve patient compliance, and reduce side effects. Our platforms include nanoparticle-based delivery, sustained-release formulations, and targeted delivery systems.

Clinical Trials

Advancing promising therapies through rigorous clinical development

Phase III

SB-291: Anti-PCSK9 Antibody for Hypercholesterolemia

A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study evaluating the efficacy and safety of SB-291 in patients with hypercholesterolemia who are at high risk for cardiovascular events.

Enrollment: 1,200 patients | Locations: 85 sites globally

Recruiting
Phase II

SB-473: Selective Kinase Inhibitor for Advanced Solid Tumors

An open-label, multi-center study assessing the efficacy, safety, and pharmacokinetics of SB-473 in patients with advanced solid tumors harboring specific genetic alterations.

Enrollment: 120 patients | Locations: 24 sites in US and EU

Ongoing
Phase II

SB-615: Novel JAK Inhibitor for Autoimmune Disorders

A randomized, placebo-controlled study evaluating the efficacy and safety of SB-615 in patients with moderate to severe rheumatoid arthritis who have had an inadequate response to conventional therapies.

Enrollment: 240 patients | Locations: 42 sites globally

Recruiting
Phase I

SB-892: Gene Therapy for Rare Lysosomal Storage Disorder

A first-in-human, open-label, dose-escalation study evaluating the safety, tolerability, and preliminary efficacy of SB-892 gene therapy in patients with a rare lysosomal storage disorder.

Enrollment: 15 patients | Locations: 5 specialized centers

Recruiting
Phase I

SB-754: Novel Antibody-Drug Conjugate for Hematologic Malignancies

A dose-escalation and expansion study evaluating the safety, pharmacokinetics, and preliminary anti-tumor activity of SB-754 in patients with relapsed or refractory hematologic malignancies.

Enrollment: 50 patients | Locations: 8 cancer centers
