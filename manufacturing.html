Quality Standards

Exceeding global regulatory requirements

GMP Certified

All our facilities adhere to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) as defined by the FDA, EMA, and WHO, ensuring consistent quality in every batch.

ISO 9001:2015

Our quality management systems are ISO 9001:2015 certified, demonstrating our commitment to customer satisfaction and continuous improvement.

ISO 14001:2015

Environmental management certification that reflects our dedication to sustainable manufacturing practices and minimizing our ecological footprint.

ICH Q10

Implementation of the International Council for Harmonisation's pharmaceutical quality system guidelines for consistent global standards.

USP Standards

Adherence to United States Pharmacopeia standards for identity, strength, quality, and purity of pharmaceutical ingredients and products.

OHSAS 18001

Occupational Health and Safety Assessment Series certification demonstrating our commitment to employee safety and wellbeing.
